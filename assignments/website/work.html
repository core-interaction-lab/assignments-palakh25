CROPPED LETTERS
Making patterns using letterforms, analysing the shape of letters to create designs. An exercise to understand positive and negative space in letter forms.
MANTRA MERCH
Choosing a personal mantra based on its meaning then designing and producing an actuale wearable item (t-shirt, tote bag, or something similar) to showcase it.
ANIMATED GIFs
Based on your chosen Mantra and design, create animated gifs. Consider factors like time, pacing,repition and motion.
MANIFESTO POSTER
Choose a historical manifesto, research the author(s) and historical context, and create a typographic poster. Explore the use of typography, grid, color, shape, and scale to create a compelling composition.
WIKIBOOK
Using Wikipedia’s own list of longest pages, choose a non-list based entry of at least 10,000 words whose content/subject fascinates you. Please use the entry’s complete text as the con- tent for a book, carefully considering what it means to translate information from one medium to another, and of course what it means to translate the ideas within your content into visual form.
